# Sniptale Code of Conduct

Sniptale contributors are expected to keep technical collaboration safe, direct, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence without personal attacks or harassment.
- Respect differences in experience, identity, background, and communication style.
- Give actionable feedback about the work, its risks, and its proof.
- Protect private information and stop sharing material when consent or provenance is unclear.
- Accept proportionate moderation intended to keep collaboration productive.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and retaliation for a good-faith conduct report are not allowed in project spaces or when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Lev Rozhkov is the accountable enforcement owner. Send conduct concerns to `levrozhkov@gmail.com`. Reports will be handled as privately as practical; conflicts of interest must be disclosed, and an affected participant must not make the enforcement decision.

Responses may include a private correction, content removal, a temporary participation limit, or a permanent ban. Decisions consider severity, repetition, impact, willingness to repair harm, and protection against retaliation.

This policy applies to repository collaboration, review discussions, project events, and public representation of Sniptale.
